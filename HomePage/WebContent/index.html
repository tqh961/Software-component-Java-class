Space

Outer space is an area of universe that is beyond the earth and between celestial bodies. Many people may think that outer space is just a vacuum that has nothing in it. However, this is a misconception. The outer space contained:

  • Low density of particles, eg. a plasma of hydrogen and helium
  • Electromagnetic radiation
  • Magnetic fields
  • Neutrinos
  • Dust
  • Cosmic rays
  • etc.

There are different regions in the outer space.

Name of region Area
Geospace The region of outer space near Earth. This include the upper atmosphere and magnetosphere.
Cislunar space The region outside the earth's atmosphere and extending out to the moon's orbit.
Interplanetary space This region is created by the release of solar wind from the sun. The atmosphere created by the solar wind is called heliosphere, which has billions of kilometers in the space.
Interstellar space This is the physical space within a galaxy.
Intergalactic space The physical space between galaxies.
